Making Bread

Combine 3 parts flour with 2 parts water to get 5 parts of pure dough.

From there, you can either cut it, bake or flatten it.

When it comes to cutting, you can cut pieces as much as you want as long as total parts equals 10.

If you bake dough, it will turn into white bread which you can cut into burger buns.

If you bake dough with 30 or more parts dough, it will turn into a loaf of bread.

If you bake flat dough, it will turn into flat dough which you can cut into flat bread. Note: the amount of parts doesn't seem to matter here.
