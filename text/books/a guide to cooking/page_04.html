Making Cake

Combine 15 parts flour, 5 parts sugar, 5 parts egg whites, 5 parts egg yolk to get 30 parts of cake batter.

From there, you can bake it giving you time to make the icing.

When it comes to icing, there are two types, one by combining 1 part corn flour 1 part sugar for 1 icing sugar, and the other by combining 1 part icing sugar and 1 part water for 1 part white icing.

If you bake cake dough, it will turn into a very nice cake!
